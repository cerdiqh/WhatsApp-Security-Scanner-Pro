🚨 Top WhatsApp Scams Targeting Nigerian Businesses

⚠️
Fake Bulk Buyer (Lagos Special): Scammer claims to be from Lagos/Abuja wanting large quantities. Creates urgency with travel stories. Requests account details immediately.
⚠️
Government Contract Scam: Pretends to be government contractor needing supplies urgently. Uses official-sounding language and fake letterheads.
⚠️
Foreign Buyer Fraud: Claims to be international buyer. Sends fake payment confirmations from foreign banks that will "take time to reflect."
⚠️
Overpayment + Courier Scam: "Accidentally" overpays, asks for refund to "courier" who will collect goods. Both buyer and courier are same scammer.

🛡️ Nigerian Business Protection Strategies

✅
The Lagos Rule: If someone claims to be from Lagos/Abuja but can't video call or provide verifiable business address, be suspicious.
✅
Bank Confirmation Protocol: Never release goods until your bank confirms payment. Screenshots can be faked easily.
✅
WhatsApp Business Verification: Only deal with WhatsApp Business accounts for large orders. Personal accounts are red flags.
✅
Local Pickup Preference: For new customers, suggest pickup from your location. Scammers rarely agree to this.
